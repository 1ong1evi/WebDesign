Hi, I'm Levi Long, a Junior at Texas State majoring in digital media innovations in the Mass Communications department.

Projects

Welcome to my website! This page is where I showcase some of my photography. The shots I am showing are a few of my favorites. However, it is hard for me to pick a favorite. I have taken over four hundred photos and have about 120 showing on my Instagram. Hope you find something that pleases your eye.

Sand

Once I tell you what state this photo was taken it will blow your mind. When I was driving up on this I thought it was of another world. This was taken at the Great Sand Dunes National Park in Colorado. If you ever have the chance to see the tallest dunes in North America do it. It was an amazing experience and a blast to photograph.

Plant

To bring a setback from landscape photography I wanted to show some different shots. This here is a picture of a Money Tree. While I rely on more landscape shots I do have a few other types. The landscape is easier for my eye to capture, so it takes these experimental ones a little longer. The camera focus on the green and the blurry white background gives the photo a beautiful meaning to interpret.

Where I've Been

Being twenty-one years old I wish I have traveled a little more. However, I would say I have been to a good amount of places. Been to the greater Los Angelos area with places such as Huntington Beach. Been almost all over Colorado from Springfield to Steamboat. Recently I just took a trip to Wisconsin which was beautiful.

Colorado

Colorado was a green dreamland. I have never seen so much green nature in my life and it won't be my last. It was such a beautiful country for about a day's drive. It makes you stop and wonder how much beauty there is in America. Coming from rocky and somewhat flat Texas it was a sight for sore eyes.

Wisconsin

Took a trip up to La Crosse, Wisconsin to see my partner and I wasn't disappointed. She told me how neat it was up there but I wasn't too sure. It was fascinating to see nature and old architecture in one place. You would look out your window and see a beautiful fall tree then to the right would be a building that is one hundred-plus year old. It is a little gem of a state that deserves a lot more appreciation.

Analytics of Instagram

In the data visualization, I am showcasing analytics of the last thirty days of reach in my stories. Reach means the number of unique users that have seen my Instagram content. Among the data, the average number of unique viewers was 87. Compared to my 481 followers it was just under a fourth of my total following. October 9th was my best day with 148 unique views.

Expanding my Photography

I've noticed that still photos are not the big hype anymore. With the recent craze of TikTok and its massive growth over the few years. I would have to catch up with the game to mix in other content ideas if I wish to grow faster. This idea and my photography only being on Instagram I think I could only grow. By reaching out to other platforms such as Pinterest, and VSCO, or even uploading to a free-use website like Unsplash I could only benefit.

Ways I can Improve

One way I can improve my reach and overall following would be consistency. I've noticed I had the most unique likes on my posts when I was uploading about once to twice a week. This also included the use of various hashtags. I was consistently hitting around 30-50 likes per post that had nothing to do with who I was as Levi Long. Another route would be a focus on short-form videos of behind-the-scenes on reels.
